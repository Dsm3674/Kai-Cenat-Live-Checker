KC Live is an open-source Twitch audience-operations workspace. Please read this disclaimer so you have honest expectations about what the project is, and what it isn't.

1. Not affiliated with Twitch or any creator

KC Live is an independent project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Twitch Interactive, Amazon, Kai Cenat, or any other creator whose channel may appear in the dashboard. All trademarks, logos, and channel artwork belong to their respective owners and are used only for identification and analysis.

2. Forecasts are estimates, not predictions

Every peak-viewer forecast, growth signal, or trend chart in KC Live is a statistical estimate produced by a local machine-learning model. It is not a guarantee of what will happen. Streams are influenced by factors the model can't fully capture — guest appearances, platform-wide events, creator schedules, world news. The model will be wrong. Sometimes very wrong.

3. Not financial, business, or investment advice

KC Live is not a financial product. The peak viewer numbers, growth predictions, and activity charts should not be used as the basis for:

  • Stock or asset trading decisions
  • Sponsorship or ad-rate negotiations
  • Creator-team hiring or headcount planning
  • Any irreversible business decision

For real audience analytics, work with Twitch's official creator dashboard or a licensed analytics provider.

4. Not gambling advice

KC Live is not a betting tool. Do not use the dashboard, forecasts, or event wire to place bets on creator performance, view counts, or any platform metric. If you choose to gamble elsewhere, do so legally, with money you can afford to lose. If gambling is causing harm, the US National Council on Problem Gambling can be reached at 1-800-GAMBLER (or your local equivalent).

5. Data sources & freshness

KC Live pulls from Twitch's Helix API and a local SQLite cache. Data may be stale, rate-limited, or temporarily missing. The status indicator in the dashboard reflects current cache health, but we cannot guarantee accuracy at any given moment.

6. Creator safety & harassment

Do not use KC Live to identify, target, or harass individual creators, streamers, or viewers. The watchlist feature exists to track public stream metrics, not to surveil people. Misuse of the platform for harassment violates our Terms of Service and may also violate Twitch's own terms.

7. Beta / hobbyist status

KC Live is an active prototype and personal project. Features can break, change, or be removed without notice. Bugs exist. The ML model may produce nonsense in edge cases.
